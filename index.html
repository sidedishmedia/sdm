Who We Are?

“If it doesn’t generate ROI – we don’t want to know. Our approach is focused on scalable conversion, making sure that every penny invested is a penny well spent.”

Hello from Matt and Andrew!

SideDish Media was being dreamt up back at school when we were just eleven (although we didn’t know it then!). We always thought about how great it would be to help restaurants get more people through their doors, and fortunately, with the power of Google Ads, our dream was able to come true.

We opened for business when we were both 22, and have built up a strong base of restaurants who trust us to manage their digital ad spend.

It wasn’t always about Google Ads! We tested many other types, including social media ads.

However, it quickly became clear that Google Ads created the most bookings, hands down.

If customers are searching for YOUR restaurant category on Google, it is so important to be near the top of their results page. Being buried on page 4 is not good for business.

Now, SideDish Media is a team of seven enthusiastic individuals, working together from their offices in Soho Square, London, to leave a huge digital footprint for catering businesses across the country.

We want to make SideDish Media your main meal when it comes to digital marketing.
